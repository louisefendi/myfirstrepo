Louis Aldorio

Louis Aldorio is a 2018 graduate of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, where he majored in Political Science and minored in History. He is currently a law student at the University of Virginia School of Law. Louis is a member of the Virginia Law Review and the Virginia Law & Business Review. He is also a member of the Virginia Law Democrats and the Virginia Law Republicans. Louis is a native of New York City and is a die-hard Yankees fan. He is also an avid fan of the New York Giants and the New York Knicks. Louis is interested in pursuing a career in corporate law.
